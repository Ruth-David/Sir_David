General Questions

Sir David Couture is a premier fashion house based in Nairobi, Kenya, specializing in bespoke suits for men and women, exquisite bridal collections, and contemporary African attire. We combine traditional craftsmanship with modern design to create timeless pieces that reflect individuality and sophistication.

Bespoke refers to clothing that is completely custom-made from scratch for each individual client. This includes personal consultations, individual measurements, custom pattern creation, fabric selection, and multiple fittings to ensure a perfect fit and unique design tailored to your preferences.

We are located at Garden Chambers, opposite Jeevanjee Gardens in Nairobi, Kenya. Our studio is easily accessible via public transport and has parking available on-site. You can contact us at 0712132268 for directions or visit our Contact page for a map.

Our business hours are:
Monday - Friday: 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM
Saturday: 10:00 AM - 4:00 PM
Sunday: By appointment only
We recommend booking an appointment to ensure availability.

  • Men's bespoke suits (business, formal, wedding)

  • Bridal collections (wedding gowns, bridesmaid dresses)
  • Contemporary African attire
  • Formal wear for special occasions

Prices may vary based on fabric choice, design complexity, and additional features. We provide detailed quotes during consultation.

Process & Timeline

Our bespoke process includes:

  1. Consultation: Style discussion and fabric selection
  2. Measurements: Comprehensive body measurements
  3. Design: Creating your custom pattern
  4. First Fitting: Initial fit check and adjustments
  5. Second Fitting: Fine-tuning the fit
  6. Final Fitting: Last adjustments and completion

Timeline varies by garment type:

  • Men's suits: 4-6 weeks
  • Women's suits: 4-6 weeks
  • Bridal gowns: 8-12 weeks
  • African attire: 3-4 weeks

Rush orders may be accommodated for an additional fee, subject to availability.

Typically, 2-3 fittings are required for most garments:

  • First Fitting: Basic structure and major adjustments
  • Second Fitting: Fine-tuning fit and minor adjustments
  • Final Fitting: Final check before completion

Complex garments like wedding gowns may require additional fittings.

Yes, we can accommodate rush orders depending on our current workload and the complexity of your garment. Rush orders typically require an additional 30-50% fee and must be discussed during your initial consultation to ensure feasibility.

  • 50% deposit required to begin work
  • Balance due upon completion
  • We accept cash, bank transfer, and mobile money payments
  • Payment plans available for orders over KSH 200,000

Appointments can be cancelled or rescheduled with 24 hours notice. For orders in progress:
